## If challenged

“Did you own the whole product?” → “Not the entire product. My scope was the workflow/product area around [specific area].”

“Do you have exact metrics?” → “I would not invent a number. The metrics I used or would use were…”

“Was TPI customer-facing?” → “It was an internal product context focused on manufacturing and quality workflows.”

“Was DataCaliper a pure product startup?” → “It was a client-facing B2B software/product-delivery environment. My product work was around SaaS and enterprise workflow modules, not owning the company’s full product strategy.”

“Were you doing engineering work?” → “No, I’m framing this as product work: understanding workflows, defining requirements, prioritizing improvements, aligning stakeholders, and measuring outcomes.”
